What is FASTag

Electronic Toll
Collection via RFID

FASTag is India's electronic toll collection system using Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) technology. A reloadable tag affixed to your vehicle's windshield automatically deducts toll fees from a prepaid account, enabling seamless passage through toll plazas without stopping.

⚠️ Mandatory for All Vehicles

FASTag is compulsory for all four-wheeled vehicles in India since February 16, 2021, as per the Ministry of Road Transport & Highways. Vehicles without FASTag are subject to double toll charges. Ensure your vehicle is FASTag-enabled to avoid penalties.
